Welcome to Casa Blanca

Casa Blanca is a private, sun-drenched villa nestled in the exclusive coastal area of Costa Benissa – ideally located between the two charming towns of Moraira and Calpe on Spain’s stunning Costa Blanca. Whether youre looking to unwind by the private pool, enjoy the sea views, or explore the Costa Blanca coast, Casa Blanca is the ideal retreat for couples, families, or remote workers. The house have room for 6 people, and have been our family vacation home for 13 years. NOK 15.000 per week for high season (June through August).

Best Beaches and Coves

• Cala Baladrar (5 mins) – A beautiful, rocky cove with crystal-clear water and a chill beach bar. Great for snorkeling!

• El Portet (15 mins) – A picturesque bay in Moraira, perfect for paddleboarding or a romantic sunset swim.

• Playa de la Fustera (10 mins) – Family-friendly beach with fine sand, calm water, and nearby cafes.

Activities and experiences

  • Benissa Ecological Coastal Walk – A gentle trail connecting hidden coves and viewpoints.
  • Peñón de Ifach Natural Park (Calpe) – A dramatic rock formation rising out of the sea. Adventurous guests can hike to the top for breathtaking views.
  • Rent e-bikes or scooters to explore the coastal roads, mountains and in-land areas.
  • Book a boat trip or snorkeling tour from Moraira's marina
  • Wine tasting at a local bodega in the Jalon Valley, Xalo or Teulada
  • Moraira Market (Fridays) – Browse fresh produce, clothing, ceramics, and more.
  • Calpe Old Town – Wander the narrow streets, visit the church square, and enjoy a slower pace of life.
  • Golfing at gorgeous Club de Golf Ifach
