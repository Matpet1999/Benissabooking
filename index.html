Welcome to Casa Blanca

Casa Blanca is a private villa between the two charming towns of Moraira and Calpe on Spain's stunning Costa Blanca. Perfect for couples, families, or remote workers seeking sun, sea views, and relaxation. The villa has room for 6 people, and has been our family vacation home for 13 years. Now I want to rent out the house to people to others looking to unwind by the private pool and enjoy a bit of a sea views. Price is EUR 1.600 per 11 days for high season (May through September).

Story of the house

The house has been in our family since 2011. We have traveled in this stunning area many times and fell in love with the surroundings, the people, and all the activities available here. We wanted a permanent place to return to whenever we visited, so we decided to look for a villa. After seeing many villas for sale, we fell in love with Casa Blanca at first sight. We have a good relationship with the neighbors, many of whom live in the area full time. The villa is one of the original houses in the neighborhood, and we believe the first version was built in the 1950s. Casa Blanca was fully renovated, modernized, and extended with a pool area in 2010. Our family has spent nearly every holiday at the villa since our children were 11 and 13 years old. Since 2023, we have mostly rented it out to families through Airbnb and Finn.no. However, due to the new fees from both platforms (which add 10–20% to the total cost!), we have now decided to rent it out directly, reducing costs for our guests.

Location & How to Get There

The house is in the municiplaity of Benissa,( Costa Benissa) ideally located between the two charming towns of Moraira and Calpe on Spain’s stunning Costa Blanca. Neares airport is Alicante (Elche) Airport. We recommend renting a car or go by bus (BeniConnect) or by public transport. Alicante Airport is 1 hour drive and Valencia airport is 1,5 hour drive. Electric bikes for rent in Calpe and Moraira; an easy and fun way to get around in the area and to beaches and restaurants.

Area and our recomondations

Casa Blanca sits in a peaceful, upscale neighborhood just minutes from gorgeous beaches, cliffside walks, scenic coves, and lively restaurants. Whether you are looking to unwind with a glass of wine at sunset or explore local Spanish markets, this location offers the best of both worlds.

Activities and experiences

  • Benissa Ecological Coastal Walk – A gentle trail connecting hidden coves and viewpoints.
  • Peñón de Ifach Natural Park (Calpe) – A dramatic rock formation rising out of the sea. Adventurous guests can hike to the top for breathtaking views.
  • E-bikes or Scooters – Rent e-bikes or scooters to explore the coastal roads, mountains and in-land areas.
  • Boat Trips or Snorkeling – Book a boat trip or snorkeling tour from Moraira's marina.
  • Wine Tasting – Wine tasting at a local bodega in the Jalon Valley, Xalo or Teulada.
  • Moraira Market (Fridays) – Browse fresh produce, clothing, ceramics, and more.
  • Calpe Old Town – Wander the narrow streets, visit the church square, and enjoy a slower pace of life.
  • Golfing at Club de Golf Ifach – Play at the gorgeous Club de Golf Ifach.
